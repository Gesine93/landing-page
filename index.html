Welcome to the website of Springfield's animal shelter

Springfield's Animal Shelter is all about making sure every paw finds its perfect match. Our shelter can host about 100 animals, ensuring they have a comfortable stay until they find their forever families. If you're a Springfield resident looking to surrender an animal, we've also got you covered — just keep in mind that there's a $50 fee to help us care for it.
